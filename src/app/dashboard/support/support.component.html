FAQs

How do I create an account?

To create an account, click on the "Sign Up" button located on the top right corner of the app's homepage. Fill in the required information and click "Submit".

How do I reset my password?

To reset your password, click on the "Forgot Password" link located on the login page. Enter your email address and click "Submit". An email with instructions on how to reset your password will be sent to your registered email address.

How do I report a bug?

To report a bug, please contact our support team by filling out the contact form on this page. Please provide as much detail as possible about the issue you're experiencing.
